Pataniscas de Bacalhau

This is a very versatile typical portuguese dish, that'll certainly be a lovely addition to christmas dinners and summer afternoons alike. Light and savoury, it goes hand in hand with a cold beer, accompanied with some salad, or a nice tomato or red bean rice.

Ingredients (2 pax)

  • 1 Cod Steak (previously soaked);
  • 15ml of Olive Oil;
  • 1 small Onion;
  • 120g of Wheat Flour;
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 Lemon
  • Milk
  • Parsley
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oil for frying

Preparation

  1. Carefully remove the skin and bones from the cod, and then shred it into flakes. Place the codfish into a bowl;
  2. Cut the lemon in half and squeeze the juice into the bowl;
  3. Pour the milk over the cod until it almost covers it completely;
  4. Nicely coat the cod with the milk and then cover the bowl with cling film. Let it rest for 2 hours;
  5. Place the flour, the egg, the olive oil, the finely chopped onion and finely chopped parsley into a bowl, and add salt and pepper to taste. Mix the ingredients together to make the batter. Pro tip: You can add a bit of the water in which you soaked the cod to add some flavour;
  6. Drain the cod flakes and add them to the batter. Mix them well;
  7. Heat up the oil and deep fry the battered cod until golden brown;
  8. Season with some salt and garnish with some parsley. Serve and enjoy!
